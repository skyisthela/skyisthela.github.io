April 9th 2026

I travelled the world a little while back, around September last year. I don't remember the exact days as it was very hectic.

 

It has already been a while since I got expelled/dropped out (I got expelled because I dropped out without an official letter), I was staying over at my friend's since that was kind of my life at the time. Just doing the most random shit imaginable.

 

My friend got a gig reselling merchandise, it required him to travel to cinemas around the country to collect said merchandise, it was around 6am and we were having 'breakfast', I had not slept at all. On a whim my friend asked if I wanted to come along and I accepted. The bus ride was quite uneventful, I was incredibly tired but goddamn Malaysia is beautiful. We stopped at a gas station, I bought a pack of cookies and during the bus ride I dropped it. I was pretty broke at the time so I didn't wanna waste it and ate it anyways.

 

We arrived in like, five or six hours, at the island of Penang. I cherish those moments but they're mostly a blur. It's filled with a ton of tourists, and a lot of pretty ladies. Apparently, my friend in an attempt to meet up with his gf rode here alone only to be ignored because the girl had strict parents. These niggas came all the way here to eat McDonald's.

 

After Penang, I had the chance to go to Johor but I had been up for almost two days, so I decided to skip. Which is funny because I ended up in Johor some time later.

 

After Penang, it was Kuantan.

 

It was a beautiful place, perhaps my favourite place in all of Malaysia. The architecture there was incredibly unique, and the nature there was really pleasant.

 

I don't really remember if Kuantan came before or after Penang tbh. Regardless, these are some of the best moments in my life as of writing this.

 

We went to Kuantan with my friend's girlfriend. She's a dumb bitch TBH. Me and my friends went swimming in the AirB&B's pool and we saw a lady there, she was literally opening her legs for us but none of us were brave enough to speak to her. Oh how I dread what could've been.

 

The scenery in Kuantan was something to die for. My friend went and bought McDonald's and it got stolen by a monkey.
