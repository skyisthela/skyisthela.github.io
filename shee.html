I am lost, I consider myself a pilgrim. But my pilgrimage has no destination and no end. I am uncertain how long I will be on this journey

My manifesto and life

I('ve) indulge myself in many hobbies and interest, some have stuck some have not. This includes media, technology, politics and many more. I am fascinated by hardware and computers. I am also interested in human biology and their limitations. I believe nothing can be perfect and nothing will ever be. Perfection fundamentally conflicts with human nature. If we achieve perfection we might as well be Gods. We are not paragons and we must always remember this regardless of topics. I don't believe live is beautiful but I don't believe it's ugly either
